app design

CAN

Talk

Traditional language apps overlook regional differences, leaving learners hesitant in real interactions. To address this, my group created a high-fidelity mockup of an app focused on Canadian accent training. We identified the problem, conducted research, and developed low- and high-fidelity designs on figma. Our app features lessons for pronunciation, a chatbot, and a daily game.

website redesign

campus

pizza

This project involved redesigning a small website to improve its design, layout, and usability. The original site featured overly vibrant colors, poor font choices, and an inconsistent layout, which were refined for a more modern and user-friendly experience. The new design combines a commercial pizza place and a local cafe’s site, merging professionalism and a sense of coziness.
